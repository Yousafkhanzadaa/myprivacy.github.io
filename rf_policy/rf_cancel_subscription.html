How to Cancel Your ReadFast: AI Summary Generator Subscription

Canceling on Google Play Store (Android):

  1. Open the Google Play Store app.
  2. Tap your profile icon (usually top-right corner).
  3. Go to "Payments & subscriptions".
  4. Select "Subscriptions".
  5. Find the ReadFast: AI Summary Generator subscription and tap it.
  6. Tap "Cancel subscription" and follow the instructions.

Canceling on the Apple App Store (iPhone/iPad):

  1. Open the Settings app.
  2. Tap on your name (Apple ID) at the top.
  3. Select "Subscriptions".
  4. Find the ReadFast: AI Summary Generator subscription and tap it.
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription" and follow the instructions.

Important Note: Canceling your subscription will prevent automatic renewal. You may continue to use ReadFast: AI Summary Generator features until the end of your current billing period.
