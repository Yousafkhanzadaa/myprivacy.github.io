Account Deletion Instructions

Please read carefully before proceeding. This action cannot be undone.

Follow these steps to delete your account:

  1. Open the CharacterGPT app on your device
  2. Navigate to your profile screen
  3. Tap the menu button in the top-left corner to open the drawer
  4. Select "Delete My Account"
  5. Follow the confirmation steps to complete the deletion

What will be deleted:

  • All your chat history and messages
  • Your profile information and settings
  • All tokens and purchased content
  • Saved preferences and favorites

Important Notes:

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed
  • Tokens and purchases cannot be refunded
  • Deletion process may take up to 30 days to complete
  • Some data may be retained for legal purposes
