Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Overview

CelebsChat is an interactive AI chat application that allows users to create celebrity characters and engage in conversations with them. Users can design and customize celebrity personas for entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Authentication data through Google/Apple sign-in
  • Email address and basic profile information
  • Optional profile customization data

2.2 Content Information

  • Celebrity character customizations and settings
  • Chat messages and interactions with AI characters (stored locally)
  • User-created character descriptions and attributes

2.3 Usage Information

  • App usage patterns and analytics
  • Character creation and interaction metrics
  • Device information and settings

2.4 Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address and network information
  • App performance and error data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the CelebsChat service
  • Enable celebrity character creation and customization
  • Authenticate your identity and maintain security
  • Process token transactions and maintain your balance
  • Analyze app performance and user experience
  • Communicate important updates and information

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Chat messages and conversations with celebrity characters are stored locally on your device for privacy. We do not maintain copies of your conversations on our servers.

4.2 Character Data

Celebrity character profiles and customizations you create are stored in our secure cloud database to enable access across your devices. This data is associated with your account and can be deleted upon request.

4.3 Cloud Services

We use Firebase for:

  • User authentication
  • Token balance management
  • Celebrity character data storage
  • Basic app functionality

5. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party services for:

  • Authentication (Google, Apple)
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Payment processing

6. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of analytics
  • Export your data
